Getting Started

Getting started begins with a discovery call where our team assesses your requirements. We then prepare a tailored demonstration of the relevant solution (G Bank AI, G Insure AI, etc.), followed by a scoping workshop, proposal, and project kickoff. Contact us at info@globalits.bh or schedule a demo directly from our website.
Implementation timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance deployment typically takes 3–6 months for Phase 1. Complex multi-module implementations for large institutions can range from 6–18 months. Our rapid deployment methodology is designed to minimize disruption while ensuring quality outcomes.

Products

G Bank AI is our flagship banking ERP built on Microsoft Dynamics 365. Core modules include: General Ledger & Finance, Loan Management, Trade Finance, Treasury Management, Regulatory Reporting (Basel III/IV, IFRS 9), Core Banking Integration, and AI-powered risk analytics. The platform is designed specifically for banks, investment firms, and financial institutions in the GCC.

Implementation

We follow Microsoft's Sure Step and Agile/Waterfall hybrid methodology, adapted for financial services. This includes: Discovery & Scoping, Solution Design, Development & Configuration, User Acceptance Testing, Training, Go-Live, and Post-Implementation Support. Each phase is managed by a certified Project Manager with a dedicated functional and technical team.

Billing & Licensing

Microsoft Dynamics 365 uses a per-user, per-month subscription model. As a Microsoft Inner Circle Partner, we help clients optimize their licensing strategy to maximize value and minimize cost. Licensing typically includes Base licenses (full access) and Activity licenses (limited function users), with module-specific pricing for Finance, HR, Supply Chain, and CRM.

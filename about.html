Our Story

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Innovation

Founded over 17 years ago in Bahrain, Global iTS was built on a singular conviction: that financial institutions deserved technology that truly understood their world. We set out to bridge the gap between enterprise software and the complex realities of banking, insurance, and investment management.

Today, we are recognized as a Microsoft Inner Circle Partner — an exclusive distinction awarded to the top 1% of Microsoft partners globally — delivering Dynamics 365 implementations that power some of the region's most prominent financial institutions.

From our headquarters in Bahrain to offices across London, Riyadh, Oman, and beyond, our team of 200+ experts brings deep domain knowledge and a relentless commitment to client success.
